Delete Your Account

If you would like to delete your BeachQuest account and all associated data, please follow the steps below:

  1. Send an email to: beachquest@hotmail.com
  2. Use the subject: Delete My Account
  3. Include your account email address

We will process your request and permanently delete your account and all associated data within a reasonable timeframe.

What data is deleted?

  • Your account information
  • Visited beaches and events
  • Likes, badges, and activity data
